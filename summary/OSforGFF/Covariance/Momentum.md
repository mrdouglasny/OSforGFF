## Overview

This file defines and studies the momentum-space free propagator $1/(\lVert k \rVert^2 + m^2)$ and derives the position-space free covariance for the Gaussian Free Field in 4D Euclidean spacetime. The central result is that the regulated Fourier integral
$$C_\alpha(x,y) = \int \frac{d^4k}{(2\pi)^4}\,\frac{e^{-\alpha\lVert k\rVert^2}\,e^{-i\langle k,\,x-y\rangle}}{\lVert k\rVert^2+m^2}$$
converges as $\alpha\to 0^+$ to the explicit Bessel formula $C(x,y)=\tfrac{m}{4\pi^2\lVert x-y\rVert}K_1(m\lVert x-y\rVert)$. The proof route goes via the Schwinger (proper-time) representation, a Fubini swap, and the Gaussian Fourier transform. A substantial further section develops the weight functions $1/\sqrt{\lVert k\rVert^2+m^2}$ used in an $L^2$ embedding of the Sobolev space $H^{-1}$.
