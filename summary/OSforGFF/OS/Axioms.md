This file states the four Osterwalder-Schrader (OS) axioms characterizing Euclidean
quantum field theories that admit analytic continuation to relativistic QFTs, following
the Glimm-Jaffe formulation (Quantum Physics, pp. 89-90). The axioms are expressed as
predicates on a probability measure $d\mu$ on field configurations: OS-0 (analyticity),
OS-1 (regularity/exponential bounds), OS-2 (Euclidean invariance), OS-3 (reflection
positivity), and OS-4 (ergodicity/clustering). A bundled structure `SatisfiesAllOS`
packs all axioms into a single predicate.
